About Us

Be part of the solution, not the pollution.

Company History

Initially known as BIOMATS, we started in Panama Oeste with an innovative hair mat to collect oil and mitigate pollution in rivers. Recognizing that the main problem was inadequate oil management in restaurants, we evolved towards BIOFITLS. This advanced solution consists of an oil trap equipped with intelligent sensors that notify when the oil reaches its maximum capacity, thus optimizing maintenance intervals. With BIOFITLS, we have transformed oil management in the restaurant sector, reducing environmental impact and promoting operational sustainability.

Mission & Vission

At Biofilt, we are committed to pioneering environmental stewardship and economic efficiency in the oil industry. Our mission is to deliver cutting-edge solutions that safeguard our planet by preventing oil contamination and to empower our clients by optimizing their operational costs. We strive to avert regulatory fines and enhance resource management through innovative strategies, ensuring sustainable success for our customers and the environment.

At Biofilt, we envision a future where our innovative cooking oil trap designs become the global benchmark, revolutionizing the industry with unparalleled efficiency and a commitment to environmental sustainability. Our goal is to set new standards, leading the way for a cleaner, greener planet.
